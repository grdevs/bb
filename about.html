"HARD WORKING ALE FOR  HARD WORKING FOLK"


Borough Brewery started with Kevin and Mandy's love for genuine real ale and a devotion to traditional welsh community. Our first beers were brewed in a 2.5 barrel micro brewery in the beer garden of The Boroughs Arms pub in 2014 and since then we have expanded rapidly with a 10 barrel brewery nestled in the heart of the Pelenna Valley.

Our beer is carefully brewed using the highest quality grains and hops  to create a unique and honest flavour and aroma. We are devoted to creating quality craft ale using traditional brewing methods and the skills and knowledge of our team.

OUR TEAM

Our people are as special to us as our location and all come with a passion for quality, local produce and the historic art of brewing. Our team, customers and community are integral to our process and are at the heart of our business ethic. Every member of our team takes great pride in what they do; if we don't like the beer, whats the point in breweing it.
