IRON RUNNER
4.3% ABV


A session ale worthy of its ABV.

A brown coloured traditional bitter, blended with Roasted Barley, Cara and Crystal Malts giving the pallet a mild bitter after taste derived from the finest British hops. 

I.P.A.
4.4% ABV


A true gem of an IPA, blended with the finest Maris Otter Pale Ale and Vienna Malts to give that strong taste, but at only 4.4% it can be deceiving.
Finished with American Simcoe and Styrian Goldings to give a crisp clean mouth-feel.

The best yet in our collection.

PELENA VALLEY ALE
4.0% ABV

Brewed in the stunning scenery of the Pelenna Mountains this ale was the first to be brewed at our new brewery location.

This session amber is easy to drink with a biscuity aftertaste.

FULL BLAST 
4.7% ABV

As its name suggests, a flavoursome straw coloured hoppy bitter using the finest Maris Otter pale ale malts.

Floral and Zesty notes are blended with Simcoe and American Cascade hops and bittered with New Zealand Waimea.

NUT RED COKE 
4.8% ABV

A Ruby Red coloured beer, with a smooth malt burnt finish, for those that like an excellent traditional ale on a long winter night.

Steeped in a rich and complicated grain bill of Maris Otter, Chocolate, Crystal, Caracoled, Roast Barley and Wheat Malts to name a few. Bittered with the finest EKG and Challenger Hops.


PIT HEAD PORTER
5.4% ABV


A dark and rich intense Porter with hints of roasted coffee and chocolate, our most treasured creation that is in a class of its own.

Maris Otter, Crystal, Chocolate Malts and Roasted Barley are blended to give truly outstanding taste to the pallet.

Not for the feint hearted.
